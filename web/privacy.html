Privacy and data use

TL Helper is an unofficial community fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by NCSOFT or Amazon Games. Throne and Liberty names, images, and game assets belong to their respective owners.

Local builds and progress

Your Armory builds, saved builds, tracker state, achievement progress, and character images are stored in your browser on your device. The anonymous beta does not require an account and does not send this local data to TL Helper.

Questlog imports

If you choose to import a public Questlog character link, TL Helper sends that link to its same-origin import endpoint. The endpoint validates the host, requests the corresponding public character, skill, and mastery data from Questlog, and briefly caches the result to protect the upstream service. Arbitrary URL query parameters are discarded.

Third-party services

  • Questlog is contacted only when you initiate an import.
  • Google Fonts may receive standard network information such as your IP address and user agent when fonts load.
  • The hosting provider may retain ordinary security and request logs.

Deleting your data

You can remove saved builds inside TL Helper or clear this site's browser storage through your browser settings. Because anonymous build data remains on your device, TL Helper cannot recover it after deletion.

Accounts

Optional account sync is not active in the anonymous beta. This notice will be updated before cloud synchronization is enabled.
