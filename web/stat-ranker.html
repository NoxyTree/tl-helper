Pick an equipment slot and up to 3 stats. Every item in the slot is scored with the same stat engine the Armory uses, normalized so large-number stats (like Max HP) don't drown out small-number stats (like Critical Hit).

Ranked items

How the score works: for each chosen stat, an item's value is divided by the best value any item in the current pool reaches for that stat (so every stat contributes on a 0–100% scale regardless of its raw magnitude). The final score is the average across your chosen stats. Values shown per stat are the raw engine numbers at the item's max level, with traits maxed.
